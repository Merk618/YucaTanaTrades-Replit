# Meridian Identity Direction

## Decision

YucaTanaTrades should preserve the organized structure of the Replit version, but restore and elevate the Meridian Intelligence Center identity.

The Replit version has strong organization, reusable structure, and a clearer app foundation. That structure should remain. The product should not drift into the visual language of common social-media fintech dashboards or generic SaaS templates. The final product needs to feel unique, premium, and one-of-a-kind.

## Product Identity

Primary product name:

- YucaTanaTrades

Identity system:

- Meridian Intelligence Center

Design principle:

- Keep Replit's structure. Bring back Meridian's soul.

## Product Feel

The interface should feel like a premium AI market command system, not a template.

YucaTanaTrades should communicate intelligence, confidence, and controlled complexity. It should feel like an AI investing cockpit built for decision-making, situational awareness, and disciplined market action.

Do not create a generic fintech SaaS dashboard.

## Preserve From The Replit Version

Keep:

- Clean app structure
- Reusable components
- Responsive layout
- Readable cards
- Current repo organization
- GitHub Pages deployment setup

The Replit version's organization is a product asset. Future design work should improve identity and presence without undoing the app's practical structure.

## Restore And Elevate From Meridian

Bring back:

- Meridian Intelligence Center branding
- Command-center feel
- Cinematic but professional visual hierarchy
- Distinctive market intelligence modules
- Gold/cyan premium data-intelligence palette
- Stronger AI market cockpit personality

The visual direction should feel premium and technical, with a sense of focus and depth. It should suggest advanced intelligence without becoming loud, gimmicky, or ornamental.

## Signature Modules To Design Later

Future product design should define distinctive modules that make YucaTanaTrades feel like its own category:

- Meridian Command Map
- Alpha Radar
- Risk Cockpit
- AI Briefing Terminal
- Conviction Engine

These modules should feel purpose-built for market intelligence rather than renamed versions of common dashboard widgets.

## Avoid

Avoid:

- Generic SaaS card grids
- Common dark dashboard templates
- Crypto-bro neon
- Fake Bloomberg clones
- Overused TradingView layout clones
- Excessive redesign before deployment is stable

The goal is not to copy existing finance tools. The goal is to learn from the clarity of strong investing products while building a distinct product language for AI-assisted market command.

## Long-Term Design Goal

Create a one-of-a-kind AI investing cockpit that feels like Robinhood, TradingView, Bloomberg, and Apple Vision Pro evolved into a new product category.

This should be a premium market intelligence environment: structured enough to use daily, cinematic enough to feel memorable, and distinct enough that it could only be YucaTanaTrades.
